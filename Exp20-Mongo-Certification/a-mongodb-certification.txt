Experiment 20: Complete the MongoDB Certification from MongoDB University

Theory:
MongoDB University offers free online courses and certifications for MongoDB. Completing a certification demonstrates your knowledge and skills in MongoDB.

Steps:
1. Go to https://learn.mongodb.com/learning-paths/introduction-to-mongodb
2. Sign up or log in with your account.
3. Enroll in the "Introduction to MongoDB" course or any relevant certification path.
4. Complete all modules, quizzes, and final assessment.
5. Download your certificate upon completion and add it to your resume or LinkedIn profile.

This step is a self-learning and certification task. No code is required, but you should keep your certificate as proof of completion.
